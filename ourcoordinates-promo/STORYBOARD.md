# OurCoordinates - Cinematic Promo Storyboard

## Overview
A 7-scene cinematic narrative following a woman who types in her special location, finds the coordinates with joy and nostalgia, then receives a hand-engraved coordinates bracelet. Portrait format (1080x1920), total duration ~22.8s.

## Scene 1: Typing (0s - 4.5s)
**File:** `compositions/scene-01-typing.html`
**Concept:** iPhone device mockup with search bar. Character-by-character typing animation shows "Central Park, New York" being entered. Coordinates "40.7128 N, 74.0060 W" appear below with golden stagger reveal. Dark background with drifting gold particles.
**SFX:** impact-bass-1.mp3 at start
**Transition out:** Blur crossfade (calm) into Scene 2

## Scene 2: Coordinates / Nostalgia (3.7s - 7.7s)
**File:** `compositions/scene-02-coordinates.html`
**Concept:** Large golden coordinates text centered with warm glow. The wanderer lifestyle image below with Ken Burns slow zoom in a rounded frame. "Every place tells a story" in Playfair Display Italic. Warm radial gold glow pulsing behind. Floating gold particles.
**Transition out:** Focus pull into Scene 3

## Scene 3: Engraving (6.7s - 11.2s)
**File:** `compositions/scene-03-engraving.html`
**Concept:** The engraved silver bracelet image positioned center-right with perspective tilt and slow Ken Burns. SVG path drawing animation simulating engraving strokes with stroke-dasharray technique. Gold sparks emanating from the engraving point. "Hand Engraved With Love" text.
**SFX:** chime.mp3 at product reveal
**Transition out:** Blur crossfade (calm) into Scene 4

## Scene 4: Packaging (10.4s - 13.9s)
**File:** `compositions/scene-04-packaging.html`
**Concept:** The gift-box-animation.gif centered in a premium gold-bordered frame. Radial gold glow behind. "Carefully Packaged" above in Playfair Display Italic. Gold ribbon accent decoratives.
**Transition out:** Blur crossfade (calm) into Scene 5

## Scene 5: Unboxing (13.1s - 17.1s)
**File:** `compositions/scene-05-unboxing.html`
**Concept:** High-quality product image (img-9343-1500x.jpg) revealed via CSS 3D card flip animation simulating box opening. Gold celebration particles burst outward. "A Moment of Pure Joy" in Playfair Display Italic. Warm radial glow intensifies.
**SFX:** chime.mp3 at reveal

**Transition out:** Focus pull into Scene 6

## Scene 6: On Wrist (16.1s - 19.6s)
**File:** `compositions/scene-06-wrist.html`
**Concept:** Collection image shown at scale with gold shimmer overlay. Subtle parallax depth layers. "Wear Your Story" in Playfair Display Italic with coordinates in RM Neue below. Gold accent lines animate in.
**Transition out:** Light leak into Scene 7

## Scene 7: CTA (18.8s - 22.8s)
**File:** `compositions/scene-07-cta.html`
**Concept:** Final CTA screen. Brand logo at top. "Find Your Coordinates Today" in large Playfair Display Bold Italic. "OurCoordinates.com" in Inter below. "BUY 1 GET 1 FREE + FREE SHIPPING" offer badge. Deep gold radial glow pulsing. All elements entrance-animate with stagger. Fades to black at end (only scene with exit animation).
**SFX:** sparkle.mp3 at CTA appearance
